Our Story

Built on Trust, Grown by Service

Founded in 2005 by the Krishna family in Rajkot, Gujarat, Krishna Enterprises began as a small stationery shop with a vision to serve local retailers with quality products at fair prices.

Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted stationery wholesalers in Saurashtra, supplying over 500 retailers, schools, offices, and businesses with a catalog of more than 1,000 products.

Our commitment is simple — quality products, honest pricing, and service that puts our customers first. Every order, big or small, gets the same care and attention.
